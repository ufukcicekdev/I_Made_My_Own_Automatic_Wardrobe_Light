## ðŸ”Œ How It Works

The batteries power the LEDs directly.
When the wardrobe door is opened, the mechanical button/trigger is released and the LEDs turn ON.
When the door is closed, the button is pressed and the LEDs turn OFF.

This makes it very simple, reliable, and power efficient.

## ðŸ”¨ Assembly Steps

1. 3D print the case from the provided STL files
2. Place the battery and spring into the holder
3. Solder the LEDs to the power wires
4. Place the LEDs facing outward
5. Install the mechanical trigger/button
6. Close the housing
7. Attach to your wardrobe or drawer
